# The Royal Circle - Heritage & Creative Community Form

A professional and elegant form for connecting creatives, entrepreneurs, and cultural enthusiasts at The Royal Circle community.

## About

The Royal Circle is a cultural and business community dedicated to:
- **Connect** with creatives and entrepreneurs
- **Create** heritage-inspired projects
- **Reform** the gap between culture and commerce

## Features

- Professional and elegant design
- Heritage-inspired aesthetic with Cinzel typography
- Responsive form layout
- Interactive form validation
- Beautiful color scheme (#722f37 and #ede8d0)
- Smooth animations and transitions

## Form Fields

- Full Name
- Contact Number
- City / Locality
- From Jaipur? (Yes/No)
- Your Heritage Connection
- Creative Ventures & Projects
- Areas of Expertise (Multiple selections)

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Fill out the form with your information
3. Select your areas of expertise
4. Click "Join the Circle" to submit
